Thesis Project

After North

Music: I’ll See You When We’re Both Not So Emotional
by American Football

My Thesis Project is an animation short film that displays a culmination of my animation and storytelling studies. After North explores emotion, memory, and the quiet distance between people through the lens of a young boy’s inner world as he is suspended between life and waking after a sudden loss.
