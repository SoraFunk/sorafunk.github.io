Creative Focus

My work explores the intersection of animation, design, and narrative world‑building. I’m drawn to emotionally driven imagery that blends surreal visual language with grounded storytelling.

Projects like After North reflect this approach - cinematic pacing, symbolic imagery, and atmosphere to explore memory, loss, and inner emotional landscapes.

Across animation, illustration, and visual design, I aim to create work that feels personal, expressive, and visually striking while still communicating clearly with an audience.
